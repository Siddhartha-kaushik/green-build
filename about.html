Inspiration

In these modern days, as life continues, we do not know where we are heading. Sometimes, when we observe that the life of people on this planet has evolved with immense speed like never before, we tend to slowly understand the changes that have previously occurred that made the existing present and those that are yet to occur, which would create the future. When it comes to the point of changes, it is not surprising to note that there is a lot of unsustainable development going on. This prevents a healthy living which is necessary to support life on Earth. What do you mean by a healthy life? Well! It is very much about reducing the usage of non-renewable resources like fossil fuels, nuclear energy sources and many more resources to decrease harmful effects on the planet like global warming, major types of pollution. However, it is not easy to shift to the usage of renewable energy sources such as solar, optimally use water, follow the 3Rs as it involves a lot of installation expenditure and right domain knowledge. The main objective behind this project is to make renewable resources available for a wide range of users within an affordable pricing plan. There are also indirect ways of raising awareness towards environmental improvement besides directly investing in the solutions. Have you ever thought of donating a certain amount to any Non- Governmental Organization but were held back because you don’t know whom to trust? This platform provides solves this issue with a list of verified NGOs. This platform also attracts people who are unaware of the present scenario of the environment through exciting events and games.
